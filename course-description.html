CDV 606: A Course Description

Course No: CDV 606
Course Title: Educational Evaluation: Theory and Practice
Prerequisites: NONE
School Year/Semester Offered: SY 2022-2023, Second Semester
Course Description:

Nature and process of evaluation as these apply to education. Emphasis will be given to various theories, models, approaches and practices as these relate to accreditation, program reviews, curriculum reviews, quality assurance and others

Course Credit/Unit: 3 units
Course Outcomes:
  1. Reviewed the different concepts of educational evaluation, its nature, benefits, processes and practices such as accreditation, program reviews, curriculum reviews, quality assurances, ISO of educational programs and others.
  2. Discussed the different educational evaluation theories, models, approaches, and practices as these apply to different educational settings;
  3. Critiqued and proposed a revision in the instrument/processes or procedures in the conduct of a chosen evaluand;
  4. Designed and implement an evaluation process to an identified program in actual setting; and
  5. Developed of sense of judgment and objectivity, validity, respect of peers in the process of evaluation.
